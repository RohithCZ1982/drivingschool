Choosing Your Transmission Type

When starting to learn how to drive, it can be difficult to choose between automatic and manual transmission. The decision depends on your personal preference, confidence, and current or future work situation.

Learning to drive a manual vehicle may be more challenging, so if you prefer ease-of-use, an automatic vehicle may be more suitable for you. Whether you want to learn manual or automatic, we can help with our patient approach to ensure you're comfortable and confident.
