Our Mission

At Safe Steering, we place a huge focus on safety to ensure that our students are equipped with the best knowledge, so they can safely get from A to B. We take pride in our ability to ensure our students are confident and capable behind the wheel before sitting their test.

Whether it be automatic or manual transmission; car, moped, or motorbike, our experienced instructor will teach you how to drive and ride the right way.

How Many Lessons Will I Need?

While you must log a minimum of 50 hours of supervised driving by law, including at least 5 hours of driving at night, there is no specific rule about how many lessons you'll need to pass. However, the more time you spend behind the wheel, the more comfortable with driving you'll end up being!

In order to set yourself up for success with your practical driving test, you'll want to make sure that you've gone over, not only the rules of the road, but over driving situations, routes, and hazards that you may encounter once you become a solo driver.

The amount of lessons you might need will vary depending on a number of factors. We need to establish your driving capabilities and any previous experience you might have. If you are a first-time driver we recommend booking in a few lessons so that you can find your groove.

Once we have observed your driving we can let you know how many more lessons we would recommend before attempting your practical driving test (PDA). If you are still not feeling confident to sit your driving test, we can talk through your concerns and work on any techniques that you feel you might be struggling with.

We will always be honest with you when it comes to your progress, and if we think that you need a little extra help with a certain driving technique we will let you know. After all, we want you to pass your test just as much as you do!
