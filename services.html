Obtaining your licence can be a daunting thought, especially if you have never driven a vehicle before. The team at Safe Steering are here to help.

We help new drivers gain confidence on our busy Western Australian roads, offering both manual and automatic driving lessons. We teach new drivers the fundamentals of driving, and the safest way to operate a vehicle. Our new drivers can expect to learn the best and safest driving techniques.

What You'll Learn:

  • Operating the vehicle correctly
  • Signaling and road awareness
  • Blind spot awareness
  • Perception awareness
  • Parking maneuvers
  • Safe driving techniques

Our vehicles are fitted with a dual control system for your peace of mind, this allows our qualified instructor to take control of the vehicle if necessary. Our structured and tailored lessons will incorporate all elements of driving.

To be your best on the road, you've got to learn from the best!

When starting to learn how to drive it can be difficult to choose the transmission type that you want to learn. Many people have different opinions on which transmission you should learn to operate, but the decision is down to you!

üîÑ Automatic Transmission

  • Easier to learn and operate
  • Less coordination required
  • Focus more on road awareness
  • Great for beginners

‚öôÔ∏è Manual Transmission

  • More control over the vehicle

Choosing between driving a manual or automatic vehicle will depend on your personal preference, your confidence, and your current or future work situation. Depending on your learning style, learning to drive a manual vehicle may be more of a challenge, so, if you lean more towards ease-of-use, an automatic vehicle may be more preferential for you.

Whether you want to learn how to drive a manual, or an automatic vehicle, we can help. Our instructor will take an extremely patient approach to ensure that you are comfortable and confident in learning to drive.
